## International Collaboration

My group has close international collaboration with Princeton University, Max Planck Institutes, KAUST, University of Twente, etc. We are interested in working with researchers across turbulent flows, interfacial flows, multiphase transport, environmental flows, and computational modeling.

## Open Positions

We are open to hire PhD students and postdoctoral researchers in my group at Tsinghua University, starting from 1 August 2026.

### Postdoctoral Researchers

We welcome applicants interested in multiphase flow, turbulence, and computational method. They are also encouraged to apply for the [Shuimu Tsinghua Scholar Program](https://postdoctor.tsinghua.edu.cn/info/zxtz/2174).

### PhD Students

Excellent candidates are always encouraged to apply. Please feel free to contact Dr. Rui Yang (ruiyang.fluid@gmail.com). Projects may involve numerical simulation and theoretical modeling of multiphase and turbulent flows.

### Application Instructions

Applicants should submit the following materials:

- CV
- Academic transcripts (if applicable)
- List of publications (if applicable)
- Statement of research interests
- Contact details for references
