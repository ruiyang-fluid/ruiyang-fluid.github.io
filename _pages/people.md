## International Collaboration

My group has close international collaboration with Princeton University, Max-Planck, KAUST, UTwente etc. We are interested in working with researchers across fluid dynamics, interfacial flows, multiphase transport, environmental flows, and computational modeling.

## Open Positions

We are open to hiring PhD students and postdoctoral researchers in fluid dynamics starting from 1 August 2026.

### Postdoctoral Researchers

Postdoctoral researchers will lead independent research projects, develop advanced computational models, and contribute to high-impact publications. They are expected to collaborate actively, mentor junior researchers, and help shape the scientific direction of the group. They are encouraged to apply for the [Shuimu Tsinghua Scholar Program](https://postdoctor.tsinghua.edu.cn/info/zxtz/2174).

### PhD Students

PhD candidates will conduct original research leading to a doctoral degree. Projects may involve numerical simulations, theoretical modeling, and physics-based modeling approaches. Students will receive close supervision while being encouraged to develop independent research ideas.

### Application Instructions

Applicants should submit the following materials:

- CV
- Academic transcripts
- List of publications, if applicable
- Statement of research interests
- Contact details for references
